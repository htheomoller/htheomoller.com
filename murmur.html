Murmur: An Experiment in Collective Memory

A framework to turn conversations into queryable memories.

People talk on WhatsApp, the system listens and remembers. No forms, no data entry. Conversation becomes lasting, searchable knowledge.

It bridges where knowledge gets created (conversation) and where it needs to live (Jira, Airtable, Calendar), connecting both through semantic search and five proactive behaviors: remind, track, alert, summarize, follow up.

Murmur is not a startup. It’s a personal project, a curious investigation. The most valuable knowledge in any team is the kind that disappears. This site documents the concept, the architecture, and what it actually looks like to build this thing one step at a time.
